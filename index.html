MODERN FAMILY



SUMMARY

Modern Family is an award-winning American television comedy series that focuses on the everyday experiences of a multi-generational family living in suburban Los Angeles.
The show uses a mockumentary style, following the lives of Jay Pritchett, his second wife Gloria, their son Manny, and Jay's two adult children, Claire and Mitchell, and their
respective families. Each episode covers the family's various ups and downs, exploring themes such as parenting, relationships, and personal growth in a humorous and
relatable way. With its witty humor, endearing characters, and relatable storylines, Modern Family is considered one of the most successful and beloved sitcoms of recent years.
